Make a pizza pie with me!

DESCRIPTION

Pizza is a beloved dish made of a crispy, chewy crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of savory toppings like pepperoni, veggies, or sausage. Its versatility, whether classic or gourmet, makes it a go-to favorite for any occasion.

Ingridients

  1. Flour
  2. Water
  3. Dough

Steps to make

  • Prepare the Dough: Mix flour, yeast, water, salt, and olive oil to form a smooth dough. Let it rise for 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.
  • Shape the Dough: Roll out the dough on a floured surface into your desired shape, typically round, and adjust thickness to preference.
  • Add Toppings: Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, then layer with cheese (like mozzarella) and your favorite toppings, such as pepperoni, vegetables, or fresh basil.
  • Bake: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and bake the pizza on a hot pizza stone or baking sheet for 10-15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
