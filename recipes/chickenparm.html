Chicken parm, yum!

DESCRIPTION

Chicken Parmesan is an ultimate comfort food that combines all the best flavors and textures in one dish. The juicy, tender chicken, perfectly breaded and fried to a crisp, gets smothered in rich marinara sauce, adding a savory, tangy element that balances the whole plate. Then, of course, there's the gooey, melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses on top, which bring a creamy, salty richness that makes each bite unforgettable. Paired with pasta or on its own, chicken parm has that irresistible mix of crunch, sauce, and cheese that just hits the spot every time. It’s the perfect blend of satisfying and indulgent, making it a go-to comfort meal.

Ingridients:

  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 ounces dry bread crumbs
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • ¾ (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 2 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps to make

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a medium baking sheet.
  2. Pour egg into a small shallow bowl. Place bread crumbs in a separate shallow bowl. Dip chicken into egg, then into bread crumbs. Place coated chicken on the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink and juices run clear, about 40 minutes. Remove from the oven.
  4. Pour 1/2 of the spaghetti sauce into a 7x11-inch baking dish. Place chicken breasts over the sauce, then cover with remaining sauce. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses on top.
